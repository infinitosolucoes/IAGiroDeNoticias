Batman was once protecting Gotham City when he was attacked by a mysterious figure known as the Scarecrow. The two fight for control of Gotham City. Batman is able to defeat the Scarecrow, but the Dark Knight's mind is now heavily affected by the Scarecrow's fear toxin. Batman begins to experience hallucinations of the Scarecrow's victims, including the murder of his parents. When the Scarecrow is defeated, Batman returns to normal.
